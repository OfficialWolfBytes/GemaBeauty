Meet Leila

I’m a Texas-raised, Christian hairstylist located in Round Rock, Texas, with a passion for creating beauty that feels authentic and uplifting —— your natural look in mind. Hospitality, kindness, and integrity guide everything I do behind the chair. Whether it’s a fresh cut or a color transformation, my goal is to help you feel confident, cared for, and truly yourself. Thank you for trusting me with your hair journey—I’m honored to serve you!
