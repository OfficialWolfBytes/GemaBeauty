Late & No Show Policies

A client that is 15 minutes late or more is automatically Canceled (as it runs into the next clients scheduled appointment).

-

No Show clients will not be allowed to re-schedule unless the next appointment has been prepaid.

Cancellation Policies

48 Hours notice is required for cancellation or rescheduling.

-

Any Deposits that have been made are only refundable up to 48 hours prior to the scheduled appointment.

Product Return Policies

Clients may exchange unused and unopened products within 7 days of purchase. No Refunds.

Child(ren) & Accompany Policies

Children age 16 and under are not permitted in the suite unless accompanied by a parent or guardian.

-

Each client is allowed One additional guest due to limited space.

Satisfaction Policies

I take pride in my work and strive to ensure your satisfaction. If you are not satisfied with your service, please let me know within 7 days of your appointment. I am happy to make any necessary adjustments to ensure your experience is positive.

Payment Policies

Payment is due at the time of service. All payments are non-refundable unless otherwise stated.

-

Cash is preferred but not required.

-

All major credit cards and mobile Tap to Pay are accepted.

-

Please note there is a 2.5% platform processing fee on all credit card transactions.

-

Checks, Cash App, Zelle, Venmo, and PayPal are NOT accepted.

Thank you for supporting my business. It's You that allows me to keep my doors open and provide me with the space to do what I love.
